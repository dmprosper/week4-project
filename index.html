Gorgeous Blooming Viburnam

My gorgeous snowball viburnam is the show stopper of my spring bushes. It blooms late spring to early summer. It is 10 feet tall, and is weighed down by these wonderful showy 5 - 8 inch flowerheads, that seem to glow at night. The non-fragrant flowers emerge lime green and turn a snowy white, and attract butterflies.
