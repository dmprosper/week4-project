My Mariesii Viburnum Hedge is wonderful deciduous shrub called the Wedding Cake Bush. It grew to 10 tall with a 12 ft. spread. Every spring there are flat-topped clusters of showy white flowers. The flowers give way to red, berry-like drupes that turn black and the birds love to eat them. The dark green foliage turns a burgundy-red and purple in the fall. It is a beauty.
